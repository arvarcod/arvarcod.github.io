  • Fire Doors

TAAV Construction pty ltd is a privately owned company in Sydney that specialises in supplying and installation of Fire, Acoustic and Radiation doors and frames. Quite simply, we aim to be the best in the business whilst striving to provide the highest quality materials for fire proof doors and frames. Additionally, providing an unmatched service for the fitting and installation of doors and frames. We work tirelessly to maintain the highest grade in our products and service as defined by our philosophy to deliver unrivaled products and service to ensure a successful long term relationship with our clients.

Our workforce consists of highly trained and skilled professionals who aim to delliver world class service to every client in any situation. We as a company embrace the latest in technology ensuring the delivery of first class service for our customers.

We are proud to provide clients with a 24 Hour door replacement service, 7 days of the week. Additionally, whilst we are capable of supplying metal door frames, doors and hardware we further pride ourselves in delivering an installation service for both metal and timber door frames.
